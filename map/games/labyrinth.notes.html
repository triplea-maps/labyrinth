About:

In Labyrinth, you assume the role of a reknowned adventurer trying to discover what is in the middle of this mysterious Labyrinth out in the middle of the Great Ocean. No one who enters ever returns, so speculation runs amok!


Victory Condition:

Make it to the center...


Resources:

PU
We only use a single PU in this map, and that is to "purchase" your character
Gold
Needed to purchase items and hire retainers at the various shops scattered throughout the Labyrinth (look for the blue-roofed buildings!)
XP
Required to purchase upgrades (i.e. level-up) for your character and his/her retainers
Resolve
Represents your character's will to complete this crazy quest. Once it is gone, you lose!

Retainers:

You may, and most likely should, hire help to get you through the Labyrinth. Fortunately there are adventurers waiting to offer their services to the intrepid heroes that are brave enough to venture forth. These retainers will don the colors of their employer, and will rise in ability as they become more experieinced.

Squire
2 HP and 3/3 attack/defense
Knight
3 HP and 4/4 attack/defense
Champion
4 HP and 5/5 attack/defense

Inventory Items:

There are many items, both purchasable and discovered, that will be very helpful as you traverse the Labyrinth. These "items" are technically "units" so they will require movement just as your hero and retainers. Many of them are one time use (e.g. suicide units) so be sure to hold them back out of combat if you intend to preserve them for later use. Also, fair warning: the Labyrinth closes as you move through it, so if you leave items behind they will become inaccessible and lost!


Enemy Units (monsters and traps):

With over thirty unique enemies and traps to encounter, and to not spoil the surprise, I can't chronicle them all here. Suffice it to say that your journey will likely be fraught with peril and danger. And should you make it out, any information you have to relay to us will be most helpful!


Special Events:

There are many interactive locations in the Labyrinth such as caves, pits, ancient ruins, etc. These sites will test your resolve, as no one knows anything about them, but rumor has it you can find treasure, experience, and items to aid your quest as you progress, so I would advise checking them out if you can!


Map Option Considerations:

  • Viewing the Unit Help has spoilers so beware! (Not that big of a deal but some people prefer surprises)
  • Although its not strictly required, it is recommended that you keep your forces together, as strange things may happen if you split up in the Labyrinth...
  • It is also recommended to go into the Game menu under User Notifications and turn off the two "Show Trigger Condition Change Roll" options as this map has tons of randomization rolls
